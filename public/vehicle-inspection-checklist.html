1. Exterior Condition
PassFailItemNotes
Front bumper — no cracks, missing parts, or major damage
Rear bumper — no cracks, missing parts, or major damage
All body panels — no major dents, rust, or structural damage
Windshield — no cracks longer than 6 inches, no obstruction
All windows — open, close, and lock properly
Side mirrors — both present, adjustable, no cracks
Doors — all open, close, and latch securely
Trunk / hatch — opens, closes, and latches properly
Paint condition — no excessive peeling, fading, or oxidation

PassFailItemNotes

Horn — functional
3. Tires & Wheels
PassFailItemNotes
All 4 tires — tread depth above 3/32" (penny test)
Tire age — manufactured within last 6 years (check DOT code)
No visible sidewall damage, bulges, or punctures
Tire pressure — within manufacturer spec (check door jamb sticker)
Spare tire or tire repair kit — present and accessible
Wheels / rims — no cracks, bends, or missing lug nuts

Windshield wipers — functional, blades in good condition
Windshield washer fluid — reservoir filled
